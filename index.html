What's UnLinked?

UnLinked is a tool to help ease the downloading of your professional data you've trusted LinkedIn with. UnLinked uses the JavaScript API to access your profile data and sends it to you in a JSON format.

Why UnLinked?

Your LinkedIn data represents a lot of work; your profile of record and the connections you've made during your career. We don't believe in holding this data hostage. You should be able to back the data up to your own servers, download it and keep it safe, and have access to it even if you leave LinkedIn or something happens to the service.1

Credits, Etc

UnLinked was a product of the December 2010 Hackday/InDay, where the employees are encouraged to explore new technologies and ideas. We've been big fans of data portability, and felt it would be a huge benefit to our users if they could one-click download their professional data. The result, was this simple two page site.
